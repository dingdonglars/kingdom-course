# Quiz answers — Module 5.8

## 1. b
The wins log is a journal in commit form. Reading it sequentially from M0 to M6 puts the year on a single page. The before-Spark-Week version of you wouldn't recognise the after-M6 version. **Sit with it.**

## 2. a
Three is the smallest sample that reveals a pattern. One friend's reaction is noise; three reactions show you something real. Thirty is overload — too much feedback, too much pressure, too easy to ignore. Three is the right amount of *real*.
