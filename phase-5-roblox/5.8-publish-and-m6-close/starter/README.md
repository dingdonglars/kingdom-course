# Module 5.8 starter — publish + M6 close

No code in the starter — the artefact of this module is the published place itself, plus the journal entries you write at the end.

## What's in here

- `M6-checklist.md` — pre-publish, publish, ritual, and final-reflection checklist. Tick the boxes as you go.
- `m6-looking-back.md.template` — the prompts for the final reflection. Copy this into your repo at `journal/m6-looking-back.md` and answer in your own words.
- `wins.md.append.md` — the M6 entry template for `journal/wins.md`. Append it to your existing wins log.

## How to use it

1. Walk through `M6-checklist.md` from top to bottom. Tick things only when they're actually done; the checklist is for you, not for Lars.
2. Once the place is published, copy `wins.md.append.md` into `journal/wins.md`, fill in the URL and the date, and post the link in `#wins` on Slack.
3. Copy `m6-looking-back.md.template` into `journal/m6-looking-back.md`. Sit with it for about an hour. Honest answers beat polished ones.
4. Tag the milestone locally and push the tag, then open the M6 milestone PR with the AI-assistance section in the description.

## After

- Live Roblox URL shared with three friends.
- M6 entry in `journal/wins.md`, posted to `#wins`.
- Tag `m6-phase-5-complete` pushed.
- `journal/m6-looking-back.md` filled in.
- M6 milestone PR opened, viva booked with Lars.

## Common gotcha

Don't rush the reflection. The temptation at the end of a long course is to bang it out in fifteen minutes and call it done. The reflection is genuinely useful for future-you in a way the modules aren't — give it the hour it asks for, in one sitting, with the wins log open in another window.
