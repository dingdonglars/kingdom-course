## Why this block?

Some lessons are too good to leave out, but they don't fit *inside* a phase without breaking it. Phase 6 is the standing container — anything we want you to do *after* the main arc lands here. Each bonus is small, focused, and brag-worthy on its own.

The bonuses currently here are:

### B1 — Engine-vs-Shell on the Database (~1 week)

**Pitch:** *"Three lines of config swap the database. That's what engine-vs-shell means."*

The deliberate, anticlimactic capstone of the engine-vs-shell theme. You swap SQLite for SQL Server in three lines, run your tests, and they all stay green. Then you meet SSMS — the professional tool — and write a one-paragraph reflection. The brevity is the lesson.

[**→ Start Bonus B1**](./B1-engine-vs-shell-on-the-database/B1.1-install-localdb/lesson.md)

### B2 — Context Engineering & AI-Driven Development (~2–3 weeks)

**Pitch:** *"Drive Claude like a senior engineer drives a junior — by handing it the right context, not the right answers."*

The deep capstone for the AI Unlock you got at Module 4.0. You've been using AI for months by now. B2 names what you've been doing and sharpens it: context scaffolding, prompt scoping, reading AI output critically, comparing AI tools, and a final reflection.

[**→ Start Bonus B2**](./B2-context-engineering/B2.1-what-context-engineering-is/lesson.md)
