# Phase 6 — Bonuses

> Two short, optional bonuses you can pick up after the main arc lands.

**What you'll have at the end:** Two finished bonuses — each its own small brag. Slot whenever convenient; usually best after Phase 5 wraps.

**Estimated effort:** ~1 week (B1) plus 2–3 weeks (B2)  ·  **Wraps:** per-bonus tag, no extra milestone

---

## Why this phase?

Some lessons don't fit cleanly inside a phase but are too good to drop. Phase 6 is where they live. Both bonuses here are optional — finish them if you want; skip them if life pulls you elsewhere. Each one stands alone and ships its own small artefact.

## The two bonuses

### B1 — Engine vs Shell on the Database (~1 week)

You shipped the kingdom on SQLite back in Phase 2. B1 swaps that for SQL Server with three lines of config — and watches every test stay green. The whole point is how unremarkable this feels: the engine-vs-shell rule predicted the swap would be small, and it is. Then you meet SSMS, the professional GUI, and write a one-paragraph reflection that names what just happened.

[**→ Start Bonus B1**](./B1-engine-vs-shell-on-the-database/B1.1-install-localdb/lesson.md)

### B2 — Context Engineering (~2–3 weeks)

The deeper bonus, after the AI Unlock you got at Module 4.0. By the time you reach B2 you've been working with AI for months. B2 names what you've been doing — choosing what the AI sees before it answers — and sharpens it. Five modules: framing, scaffolding, scoping, reading output critically, and a final reflection that compares the tools.

[**→ Start Bonus B2**](./B2-context-engineering/B2.1-what-context-engineering-is/lesson.md)
